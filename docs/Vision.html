Our vision

If you feel anxious about moving day, you’re not alone. In a recent survey, moving a home was ranked life’s third most stressful experience, above childbirths and job interviews.

And it’s easy to see why. After the long and exhausting process of finding a new home, you’re suddenly faced with the task of packing and moving nearly all your possessions in a matter of days. On top of this comes the administrative load: updating your address, arranging utilities, setting up internet access, managing subscriptions, and more. In the midst of all this, it’s understandable that sustainability is not the first thing on movers’ minds. In fact, surveys suggest that almost 4 in 10 UK movers admit recycling is “the last thing” they think about during a move. As a result, over a quarter of movers reportedly throw away more than 30 recyclable or reusable items.

The environmental impact of this is enormous. We estimate that, across the UK, 90 million cardboard boxes are used every year for house moves—equivalent to more than one million trees, or around one-eighth of London’s entire tree population. Recent industry reports also estimate that £150 million worth of household waste is generated during moves each year, contributing to the more than one million tonnes of municipal and residential wood waste produced annually in the UK.

At Tulip ReMoving, our vision is to change this. We believe you deserve a zero-stress, zero-waste moving experience—one that benefits you, your belongings, and the planet. That’s why we take responsibility at every step:

  • Packing sustainably: Using reusable crates made from recycled plastic, multi-use padding, and protective equipment that we take back and use again.
  • Transporting responsibly: Operating with zero-emission electric vans, reducing the carbon footprint of every move by up to 80% compared with diesel alternatives.
  • Giving furniture a second life: Delivering unwanted furniture to resale or donation partners, ensuring it avoids landfill and helps others in need.
  • Unpacking and clearing: Once your belongings are safely delivered, we remove all packing materials to be reused for the next move—leaving your new home clutter-free from day one.

By leaving it to Tulip ReMoving to move your home, you’re helping to protect your wellbeing and our planet. We make sure the process is as sustainable, simple, and stress-free as possible, so you can focus on settling into your new home without the guilt or the hassle.
